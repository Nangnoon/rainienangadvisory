Frequently Asked Questions

Is the consultation really free?

Yes! The initial consultation is completely free with no obligations. It's an opportunity for us to understand your needs and for you to see if we're a good fit.

How long is the consultation?

Initial consultations typically last 30-45 minutes. This gives us enough time to understand your situation and discuss potential solutions.

Can we meet in person or only online?

I offer both options! We can meet at a convenient location in Singapore, or have a video call if that works better for your schedule.

What should I prepare for the consultation?

Just come with an open mind! If you have existing policies or financial documents, having them handy can be helpful, but it's not required for our initial chat.
